# TRANSACTION COSTS
The costs of buying or selling investments. These costs typically take the form of brokerage commissions, exchange fees and/or taxes, and/ or bidâ€“offer spreads from either internal or external brokers. Custodial fees charged per transaction [[SHOULD]] be considered [[CUSTODY FEE|CUSTODY FEES]] and not [[TRANSACTION COSTS]]. For [[REAL ESTATE]], [[PRIVATE EQUITY]], and other [[PRIVATE MARKET INVESTMENTS]], transaction costs include all legal, financial, advisory, and investment banking fees related to buying, selling, restructuring, and/or recapitalizing investments but do not include dead deal costs.
