# TEMPORARY NEW ACCOUNT
An account for temporarily holding client-directed [[EXTERNAL CASH FLOW|EXTERNAL CASH FLOWS]] until they are invested according to the [[COMPOSITE]] strategy or disbursed. A FIRM can use a temporary new account to remove the effect of a [[SIGNIFICANT CASH FLOW]] on a [[PORTFOLIO]].
